# Localize SaaS for China

Turn verified English SaaS product facts into an evidence-safe China market launch pack.

The skill creates natural Simplified Chinese positioning and launch material while keeping unsupported claims out of customer-facing copy. It is designed for founders, SaaS marketers, product teams, and agencies evaluating China market entry.

Service overview and public work: https://chris-saas-services.stomeonst123.chatgpt.site

## What it produces

1. Market-fit snapshot.
2. Evidence ledger and launch blockers.
3. Three Chinese positioning directions with English back translations.
4. Implementation-ready Chinese landing-page copy.
5. WeChat launch article when the channel fits.
6. Xiaohongshu concepts only when the audience genuinely fits.
7. Two bilingual sales emails.
8. Buyer objections with evidence-safe answers.
9. A fourteen-day implementation checklist.

## Safety design

The skill will not invent customers, integrations, certifications, local hosting, Chinese support, response times, pricing, regulatory status, or performance results.

It separates verified facts, user-provided facts awaiting verification, inferences, and missing evidence. Customer-facing copy uses verified facts only. It also treats mainland accessibility, data and security documentation, and the procurement path as default launch gates until current evidence resolves them.

The skill prepares drafts. It does not publish, send messages, register accounts, purchase services, request credentials, or handle payments.
