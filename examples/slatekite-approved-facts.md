# Example 1: SlateKite with approved product facts

This is a fictional example. It demonstrates how the skill uses an accountable, dated product facts sheet. It does not describe a real company, customer, or performance result.

## Example request

```text
Use $localize-saas-for-china to create the market-fit snapshot, evidence ledger, positioning directions, Chinese landing-page hero, channel recommendation, one bilingual sales email, buyer objections, and fourteen-day checklist for SlateKite Approval Hub.

SlateKite is a fictional B2B SaaS product for procurement operations teams.

Approved product facts sheet:

Owner: Product Director, SlateKite
Approved for external use: 2026-07-15

1. Purchase requests can be submitted through a web form or CSV upload.
2. Approval paths can be configured by legal entity, department, purchase category, amount, and risk level.
3. Every approval decision is made by a designated human approver.
4. The product interface and support service are available in English only.
5. Prospects can request a product demonstration by email.
6. Pricing is confirmed after discovery.
7. SlateKite has no China entity and cannot issue a Chinese fapiao.
8. No mainland accessibility test, China legal review, DPA, data-location evidence, public customer name, performance result, local payment method, or local integration list is available.

Target buyer: procurement operations leaders at multinational companies with teams in Mainland China.

Desired action: request a product demonstration by email.
```

## Representative output excerpt

### Market-fit snapshot

Product: SlateKite Approval Hub, a purchase-request and approval-path SaaS product.

Primary buyer: procurement operations leaders at multinational companies with Mainland China teams.

Most credible use case: centralize purchase requests and route them through configured approval paths while keeping each approval decision with a designated human approver.

Desired action: request an English-language product demonstration by email.

Recommended channels: Chinese landing page, WeChat educational article, and researched bilingual sales outreach. Xiaohongshu is excluded because the available buyer context is narrow enterprise procurement.

Confidence: medium. The workflow facts are approved for external use, while mainland accessibility, data documentation, and the procurement path remain unresolved.

### Evidence ledger excerpt

| Proposed claim | Status | Evidence and date | Safe wording |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Requests can enter through a web form or CSV | verified | Product Director approved facts sheet, 2026-07-15 | Submit purchase requests through a web form or CSV upload |
| Approval paths can use five defined conditions | verified | Same approved facts sheet, 2026-07-15 | Configure approval paths by legal entity, department, purchase category, amount, and risk level |
| The product supports Chinese-language operation | missing | Approved facts say the interface and support are English only | Current product use and support require English |
| The product is accessible from Mainland China | missing | No current mainland network test | Treat product, login, integrations, and demo path as launch gates |
| A Chinese buyer can contract, pay locally, and receive fapiao | missing | No China entity, local payment method, or fapiao capability | Confirm contract, payment, tax document, and invoicing requirements before procurement |

### Recommended positioning

中文标题：采购申请集中提交，审批按规则流转。

中文副标题：通过网页表单或 CSV 提交采购申请，按法人主体、部门、采购品类、金额和风险等级匹配审批路径，最终决定由指定审批人完成。

English back translation: Submit purchase requests in one place and route approvals through defined rules. Use a web form or CSV to submit requests, match each request to an approval path by legal entity, department, category, amount, and risk level, and keep the final decision with a designated approver.

### Landing-page hero

标题：采购申请集中提交，审批按规则流转。

副标题：将采购申请和审批路径放到同一工作台。每一项审批决定仍由指定审批人完成。

主按钮：邮件申请产品演示

按钮旁说明：当前产品界面和支持服务使用英文。大陆访问、数据要求和采购条件将在评估前确认。

### Channel decision

Use a Chinese landing page for buyer education and a WeChat article for procurement workflow explanation. Do not create Xiaohongshu content without a genuine practitioner or small-business angle.

### Bilingual first-touch email

The recipient signal stays visible until researched.

```text

The current product and support service operate in English. We would confirm mainland access, data, integration, and procurement requirements before any rollout.

Would a short product demonstration be useful?

Best,
[Sender]
```

### Default launch gates

1. Test the product, login, critical integrations, and demo path from Mainland China.
2. Obtain data-flow, retention, subprocessor, security, and contractual documentation.
3. Confirm the contract entity, payment route, tax documents, invoicing expectations, and procurement owner.

### Final quality summary

Ready to implement: positioning draft, landing-page draft, channel plan, and email template.

Requires verification: mainland accessibility, data documentation, contract and payment path, local integration support, and recipient-specific sales signals.

Deliberately excluded: China compliance claims, local hosting, Chinese-language support, named customers, performance results, local payment, and fapiao promises.
